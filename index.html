About US

Set in the heart of Shevington,
Wigan, we are a local independent
restaurant using fresh, seasonal, local produce.

Whether you’re looking for a lighter bite for lunch, or for somewhere to enjoy a meal with family or friends in the evening, why not come and book a table with us. We offer a selection of classic and speciality dishes, including delicious options for our younger guests. .

Our Ingredients

We pride ourselves on using the best ingredients we can source, especially the ones right on our doorstep! We keep it as local as we can, and follow the seasons to make sure only the best goes into our food

Our Suppliers

Our suppliers mean the world to us and many of them have worked with us for over a decade. They tell us what’s best currently, and what we’ve got to look forward to!

Our Dream

We’re hoping to create something special in Shevington, bringing our knowledge and passion to your doorstep. We want a friendly welcome to await you, whether it’s for a relaxing lunch or something extra special in the evening.

Air Conditioning At Juniper Restaurant we have a state-of-the-art fresh air system which replenishes 500m3 of fresh air an hour from the outside into our restaurant.
